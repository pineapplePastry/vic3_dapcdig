# v1.1.0  

## Features

* Separated the Devout IG conversion into a separate diplomatic action "Change Religion of Administration", to allow overlords who have changed their religion apply that change to colonial subjects that have already adopted the overlord's primary cultures

* "Change Religion of Administration" and "Force State Religion" now have the same effects, the difference being activation requirements (the former only usable on colonial subjects, the latter on junior bloc members of a Religious Convocation power bloc that you are the leader of)

* Atheist overlords now can spread Atheism to their subjects or bloc members, as the religious diplomatic actions in this case will now immediately enact the "State Atheism" law in the target country (but will leave the Devout untouched)

* Converting an Atheist country will immediately enact "Freedom of Conscience" so that conversion can begin

*  Rulers and Heirs will now convert religions with the Devout interest group, however other characters affiliated with the Devout will be retired if their religion does not match the new religion

## Balance
* "Change Language of Administration", "Change Religion of Administration" now convert 5% of pops **nationwide** to the target culture/religion, to allow assimilation and conversion to start

* The assimilation bonus for "Change Language of Administration" was removed, and the bureaucracy and institution change speed malus is now also applied nationwide

* Converting Devout interest groups now also radicalises 10% of pops belonging to the previous religion

* "Change Language of Administration" now converts **50%** of bureaucrats in the target country to the new primary culture (determined by the largest population that is a primary culture in the overlord's country), and immediately enacts the "Appointed Bureaucrats" law
